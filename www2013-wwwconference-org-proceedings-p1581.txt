 in a graphical password, a user interacts with one or more images to create or enter a password. several password discretization schemes have been proposed. when creating a password, one of the three grids is selected for each click-point. a password consists of multiple click-points. these guesses are then sorted by their likelihood of being the password.  password consists of 5 click-points in our studies. adversaries do not have access to any password or any of the remaining images any user uses.
